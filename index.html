Hello, I'm Konishi. That's the name I go by nowadays with creative works. I'm an illustration and design hobbyist, and this website serves as a general demonstration of my works.

According to my boss, I'm "a coder by day, an artist by night". Visual art has been a mean of expressions for me since at a very young age. I've been exposed to many genres of art, and also have varied experiences creating them with different tools.

A great amount of works were made when I was younger, because that's when I had the most time with hobbies outside of school, hah! My works won several awards during school-wide competitions, and a few Chinese watercolour paintings were chosen to be included in the provincial juniour high art textbook.

My current digital tools of trade are, but not exclusively: Photoshop, Paintool SAI, Maya, ZBrush, Flash.

If you are interested in my art works, feel free to ask me and I may be able to do commission on a case-by-case basis.

Thank you for visiting!
